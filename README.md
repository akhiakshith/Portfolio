Welcome to my master portfolio. This is an ultra-minimalist, high-end Gen Z dark-mode portfolio showcasing my credentials, professional experience as a Business Analyst / Full-Stack Developer, and key academic and personal projects.

---

## ⚡ Design System & Features

- **Obsidian Dark Aesthetic:** Designed with a deep slate background (`#080808` / `#101010`), sleek custom borders, and bright crimson (`#E8203A`) accents.
- **Interactive Particle Network:** A custom Canvas-powered background that responds to cursor movement and connects floating points dynamically.
- **Interactive Mouse Glow:** Smooth, gradient light trailing behind the cursor for depth and engagement.
- **Asynchronous Animations:** Fluid fade-and-lift entry reveals powered by `IntersectionObserver` to trigger clean transition states when scrolling.
- **Fully Responsive Navigation:** A vertical slide dock navbar for desktop screens that transitions into a sticky mobile header on touch displays.
